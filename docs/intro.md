# Welcome to Dalgo

**Our open-source data platform enables NGOs to automate data consolidation, transformation, storage and visualisation through a unified interface.**

⏰ Spend no time on repetitive manual data crunching\
📊 Direct your efforts towards the use of data to monitor and evaluate your impact\
📣 Learn, iterate and communicate your impact internally and externally

Visit [dalgo.org](https://dalgo.org/) to learn more about the product and pricing, or contact us at support@dalgo.org.\
Our team is always available on Discord. [Join our server](https://discord.gg/C7BKvYPufp) and chat with us on Dalgo Support.

## Platform Overview

[insights.dalgo.org](https://insights.dalgo.org/) is the interface for your M&E team, data analysts, or IT team. It enables you to set up and monitor automated data pipelines through the following sections:

1. **Ingest** — set up your data warehouse, connect to your data sources, and link those sources to your warehouse.
2. **Transform** — connect to your dbt repository, which contains the SQL code for cleaning, merging, and computing your data.
3. **Orchestrate** — schedule your data ingestion and transformation to run automatically.
4. **Dashboards** — explore your data through [Dalgo Native Dashboards](./analysis/dalgo-dashboards) or [Superset](./analysis/superset).
5. **Reports** — capture and share snapshots of your dashboards for routine monitoring and donor updates.
6. **Pipeline Overview** — monitor the health of your pipelines and view past run history.
7. **User Management** — add team members and assign roles to control their access.

> Note: Superset is only available if you have subscribed to the Dalgo + Superset plan.

## Impact at a Glance

When you log in, you land on the **Impact at a Glance** home screen. If a dashboard has been set as your landing page, it appears here automatically.

If no landing page is configured yet, you will see two options:
- **Choose a dashboard** — select an existing dashboard to set as your home screen
- **Create new dashboard** — build a new dashboard from scratch

To set a landing page, open any dashboard and select **Set as my landing page** from the dashboard menu.

## Getting started as a first-time user

Your data pipelines will typically already be set up for you by the Dalgo team or one of our partners. The steps below help you log in and start using the platform.

1. You will receive an invitation email from notifications@dalgo.org.

![Welcome email](/img/welcome-email.png)

2. Select the link in the email to open the Dalgo platform.
3. Accept the invitation and set up your password.
4. You are now logged in.

> If the Pipeline Overview says "No pipelines available", reach out to support@dalgo.org or the partner helping with your setup.
