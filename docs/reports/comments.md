# Executive summaries and comments

**Add written context to a report and discuss findings with your team directly on the charts.**

## Editing the executive summary

The **Executive Summary** is a free-text section at the top of every report. Use it for key takeaways, observations, or notes for reviewers.

1. Open the report you want to edit.
2. Select the **pencil (✎) icon** next to the **Executive Summary** heading.
3. Type your notes in the text area that appears.
4. Select **Save** to keep your changes, or **Cancel** to discard them.

## Commenting on charts

You can leave comments on the executive summary or on any individual chart.

1. Select the **comment icon** on the executive summary or on a chart card.

![Comment popover](/img/reports/reports_comment.png)

2. Type your comment in the text field.
3. To mention a team member, type **@** followed by their name. They will receive a notification.
4. Select **Send** to post your comment.

To edit or delete one of your own comments, select the options icon next to the comment.

:::note
Unread comments are shown with a highlighted comment icon on the chart or summary section.
:::
