# Welcome to Dalgo

**Dalgo is an open-source data platform that helps NGOs automate data collection, transformation, and visualisation — without needing a data engineering team.**

Your data lives in many places: KoboToolbox surveys, Google Sheets, CommCare, Avni, Zoho, and more. Dalgo pulls it all into one warehouse, cleans and joins it using dbt, runs everything on a schedule, and surfaces the results as charts, dashboards, and reports — all through a single interface.

Visit [dalgo.org](https://dalgo.org/) to learn more about pricing, or reach out at support@dalgo.org.\
Join our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/C7BKvYPufp) to chat with the team and the community.

## How Dalgo is organised

The left navigation mirrors your workflow:

| Section | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Impact** | Your home screen — shows your default dashboard at a glance |
| **Charts** | Create and manage the visual building blocks of your dashboards |
| **Dashboards** | Arrange charts into shareable views |
| **Reports** | Capture point-in-time snapshots of dashboards to share or review |
| **Data** | Ingest, transform, orchestrate, explore, and monitor your data |
| **Settings** | Manage users, billing, and organisation settings |
