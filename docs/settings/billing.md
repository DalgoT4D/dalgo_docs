# Billing

**The Billing page shows your organisation's current subscription and lets you manage billing details.**

## Accessing Billing

1. Select **Settings** in the left menu.
2. Select **Billing**.

![Billing page](/img/settings/settings_billing.png)

## What you'll see

- Your current **plan** and the features it includes
- Your **billing cycle** and next renewal date
- An option to **update payment details** or contact the Dalgo team about your subscription

## Upgrading your plan

To add Superset, increase usage limits, or discuss a custom plan, contact support@dalgo.org. The Dalgo team will update your subscription and confirm the changes.
