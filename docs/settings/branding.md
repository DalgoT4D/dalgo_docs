# Branding

**Branding lets you add your organisation's logo so it appears across dashboards, chart exports, and PDFs.**

## Accessing Branding

1. Select **Settings** in the left menu.
2. Select **Branding**.

![Branding page](/img/settings/branding_default.png)

## Setting your logo

You can set your logo two ways — by uploading a file, or by linking to an image already hosted online. You can only use one method at a time; to switch, remove your current logo first.

### Uploading a file

1. On the **Upload** tab, drag and drop an image onto the upload area, or select it to browse your files.
2. Supported formats are **PNG**, **JPG**, **WEBP**, and **GIF**, up to **5MB**.
3. Select **Save** to confirm.

![Branding Upload tab with a saved logo](/img/settings/branding_upload_saved.png)

### Linking an image

1. Select the **Link** tab.
2. Enter the URL of an image hosted online.

![Branding Link tab](/img/settings/branding_link_tab.png)

3. Select **Save** to confirm.

## Updating or removing your logo

Once a logo is set, the buttons under the upload area change:

- Select **Update Image** to replace the current logo with a new one.
- Select **Remove** to delete the logo entirely.

## Where your logo appears

Once saved, your logo shows up automatically across the platform:

- The app header, on every page
- Dashboard and chart exports, in PNG and PDF format
- Fullscreen chart view
- Public dashboard and report links, alongside "Powered by Dalgo"

The preview panel on the Branding page shows a live example of how your logo looks in context before you save.
