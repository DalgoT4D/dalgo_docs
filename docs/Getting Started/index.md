# Welcome to Dalgo

**Leverage Dalgo to manage your Data. So that you can Learn from it.**

Our open-source data platform enables NGOs to harness the power of data by automating data consolidation, transformation, storage and visualization through a unified interface.

This ensures that you spend no time on repetitive manual data crunching and can direct your efforts towards the use of data to monitor and evaluate your impact. Learning, iterating and communicating your impact internally and externally.

Visit [dalgo.org](https://dalgo.org/) to learn more about the product and pricing, or contact us at support@dalgo.org

Our team is always available to provide you with support via Discord. [Join our server](https://discord.gg/C7BKvYPufp) and chat with us on Dalgo Support.

## Getting Started

To get started with Dalgo, first set up your account: [Account Setup](getting-started/account-setup).

## The Data Pipeline Process

Dalgo's data pipeline consists of four main processes:

1. **[Ingest](process/ingest):** Connect your data sources to a single warehouse.
2. **[Transform](process/transform):** Apply data transformations using DBT (and UI4T in the future).
3. **[Visualization](process/visualization):** Create and view dashboards in Superset.
4. **[Orchestration](process/orchestration):** Schedule and automate your data pipelines.
