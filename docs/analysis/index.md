# Dashboards

**Dalgo supports visualisation through native dashboards and, optionally, a hosted Superset instance for more advanced dashboarding.**

## Visualisation options

Within your Dalgo subscription, you can use:

1. [**Dalgo Native Dashboards**](./dalgo-dashboards) — build charts and dashboards directly inside Dalgo, with no external tool required.
2. [**Superset**](./superset) — a hosted Superset instance for customisable, role-based visualisations. Available to organisations on the Dalgo + Superset plan.

## Connect your own BI tool

Dalgo also works with any external BI or dashboarding tool of your choice. Connect your data warehouse to your preferred tool — such as Tableau, Looker, Power BI, or Metabase — and start building.

> **Note:** Dalgo does not manage or host external BI tools. You bring your own licence or account and connect it to the warehouse that Dalgo pipelines into.
