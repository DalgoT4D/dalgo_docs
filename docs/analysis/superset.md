
# Superset 

1. Dalgo's native dashboards are available directly within the platform for built-in reporting and monitoring.
2. If you have not subscribed to Superset, you will see a message to this effect. Kindly contact support@dalgo.org if you wish to add Superset to your subscription.
3. If you have subscribed to Superset, you will see a button for Google sign-in. Click on it.

<img width="1464" alt="N6 2) DSS sign in" src="https://github.com/DalgoT4D/dalgo_docs/assets/119285990/afe2d22c-14de-47f8-8acd-8417643b6220" />

4. A pop-up window will appear. Select Sign In with Google.

<img width="1470" alt="NN6 3 gogle sign in" src="https://github.com/DalgoT4D/dalgo_docs/assets/119285990/69b470be-5cbc-4f98-84f7-94e7f29431b2" />

5. If your Superset admin has granted access to your email ID, you will be successfully logged in. Otherwise, contact your Superset admin.
6. Once successfully logged in, close the pop-up window. You will now be able to access Superset via Dalgo to build charts and monitor whether your visualizations are being populated as expected.
