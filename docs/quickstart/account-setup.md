# Setting up your account

**You'll receive an email invitation from notifications@dalgo.org. This page walks you through accepting it and logging in for the first time.**

## Steps

1. Open the invitation email from **notifications@dalgo.org**.

![Welcome email](/img/welcome-email.png)

2. Select the link in the email. Your browser will open the Dalgo platform.
3. Enter a password and confirm it. Select **Accept Invitation**.
4. You are now logged in and will land on your **Impact at a Glance** home screen.

:::note
If you don't see the invitation email, check your spam folder. If it still isn't there, ask the person who set up your Dalgo workspace to resend the invitation from **Settings → User Management**.
:::
