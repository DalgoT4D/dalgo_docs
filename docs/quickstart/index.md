# Quickstart

**New to Dalgo? This five-step walkthrough takes you from your invitation email to your first report — in under an hour.**

Each step is one screen. At the end of each step you'll find a link to the deeper reference page if you want more detail.

## Steps

1. [Setting up your account](./account-setup.md) — accept your invitation and log in for the first time
2. [Your home: Impact at a Glance](./impact.md) — understand what you're looking at when you first sign in
3. [Viewing your first dashboard](./first-dashboard.md) — open a dashboard and read its charts
4. [Creating your first report](./first-report.md) — capture a snapshot to share with your team
5. [Where to go next](./next-steps.md) — a map of what to explore depending on your role
