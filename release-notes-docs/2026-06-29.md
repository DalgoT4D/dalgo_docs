# June 29, 2026

Your logo just moved into Dalgo. It now rides along on chart exports, PDFs,
fullscreen views, and shared dashboards.

## Organization branding

You can now add your organization's logo to Dalgo so it appears on your
exports and public-facing views.

- Upload a logo (up to 5 MB) or point Dalgo at a hosted image URL, from
  **Settings → Branding**.
- Your logo, together with a small **Powered by Dalgo** mark, shows up on:
  - Chart and table exports (image and PDF)
  - Map chart exports
  - Fullscreen chart and dashboard views
  - The header of dashboards shared publicly

## Consistent number formatting for KPIs

KPIs now use the same number format options everywhere they appear — on the
KPI page itself, in KPI cards inside reports, and in KPI-based alerts. Format a
KPI once and it stays consistent across the product.

## Fixes

- **Dashboard tabs** — you can now rename a dashboard tab even when it's the
  only tab on the dashboard.
- **Chart metrics** — fixed an issue that prevented metrics from being saved
  to the metric library.
- **Drill-down** — tightened the permission checks that run when editing a
  chart from a drill-down view.
