title: Dalgo login
---

1. Please check your email for an invite to join the Dalgo platform similar to below:  
   ![Screenshot](./images/local-dev-setup-image1.png)  
2. Click on the attached link in the email to set your password.  
3. Go to [https://insights.dalgo.org/](https://insights.dalgo.org/) to find Dalgo v2, our revamped Dalgo experience.  
4. You should be automatically logged into Dalgo v2. If not, kindly login using your credentials.
